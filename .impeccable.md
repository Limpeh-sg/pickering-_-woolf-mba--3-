## Design Context

### Users
Prospective MBA applicants and working professionals comparing a flexible online MBA against career, family, and budget constraints. They need to quickly understand credibility, recognition, curriculum, fees, intake timing, and how to speak with admissions.

### Brand Personality
Confident, international, and polished. The experience should feel premium and trustworthy without becoming flashy, gimmicky, or overly decorative.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined education landing page with a blue-led institutional palette, crisp editorial typography, generous spacing, and smooth scroll-triggered motion inspired by the referenced MBA landing page. Motion should feel like calm momentum: staggered reveals, subtle parallax, hover lift, icon feedback, and clear state transitions.

### Design Principles
- Lead with credibility and clarity before visual flourish.
- Use motion to guide attention and explain hierarchy, not to decorate every element.
- Keep interactions fast, smooth, and respectful of reduced-motion preferences.
- Preserve the existing Pickering and Woolf brand assets and bilingual content structure.
- Make high-intent actions such as Apply, Consult, and Brochure feel responsive and easy to find.
